# Beacon 400 Headlamp

- Product ID: LGT-001
- Category: Electronics
- Price: CHF 49
- Availability: in stock
- Customer rating: 4.5 / 5

A rechargeable headlamp for anyone who hikes into dusk or moves around camp
after dark. It throws up to 400 lumens — bright enough to pick out a trail
— and weighs just 78 grams on your forehead.

A red night mode keeps your eyes adjusted to the dark and avoids dazzling
tent-mates, while a low setting stretches the battery to about sixty hours
for trips far from a power socket. It charges over USB from a power bank.
