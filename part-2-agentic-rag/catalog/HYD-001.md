# Glacier 1L Insulated Bottle

- Product ID: HYD-001
- Category: Hydration
- Price: CHF 35
- Availability: in stock
- Customer rating: 4.8 / 5

A one-litre stainless-steel bottle with a vacuum-insulated wall that holds
temperature for a remarkably long time: cold drinks stay cold for around 24
hours, and a hot drink is still hot some 12 hours later.

That makes it as useful for ice water on a summer hike as for tea on a
frosty winter walk. The steel construction shrugs off dents and does not
hold flavours, and a leak-proof cap lets you drop it into a pack next to
your down jacket without worry.
