# Rivercross Trail Runner

- Product ID: FTW-002
- Category: Footwear
- Price: CHF 149
- Availability: in stock
- Customer rating: 4.4 / 5

A fast, light shoe for runners and fast-packers who would rather feel the
trail than be insulated from it. At 280 grams per shoe the Rivercross is a
fraction of the weight of a hiking boot, with a flexible sole and a
breathable mesh upper.

That mesh is deliberate: this shoe is built to get wet and drain quickly,
so stream crossings and rain do not leave you squelching for hours. It
trades the ankle support and durability of a boot for speed and comfort on
runnable ground.
