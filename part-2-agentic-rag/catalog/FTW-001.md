A mid-cut boot for hikers who want their feet to stay dry and supported on
rough ground. A waterproof-breathable membrane keeps puddles, wet grass and
light snow out, while the cuff rises above the ankle to steady you under a
heavy pack on loose, uneven trails.

The Vibram outsole bites into mud and wet rock, and a protective rand
shields your toes against the knocks that come with rocky terrain. Best
broken in on a few short walks before a long tour.
