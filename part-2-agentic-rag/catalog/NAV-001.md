# Pathfinder GPS Watch

- Product ID: NAV-001
- Category: Electronics
- Price: CHF 329
- Availability: in stock
- Customer rating: 4.4 / 5

A wrist-worn navigator for hikers who want to know exactly where they are
without stopping to unfold a map. The Pathfinder shows topographic maps on
its display, records your route as you walk, and guides you back along it
if the trail disappears.

A barometric altimeter tracks elevation gain and warns of the pressure
drops that often precede bad weather. Battery life reaches forty hours in
full-tracking mode — enough for a long weekend of navigation before it
needs a charge.
