Warmth with almost no weight. The Featherlight is filled with 800-fill-power
goose down that traps a remarkable amount of body heat for just 320 grams,
and it stuffs into its own chest pocket to the size of a water bottle.

This is a cold-and-dry-weather piece: down loses its loft once it gets
soaked, so on a wet day wear it under the Stormshield rather than on its
own. On crisp winter mornings, frosty summit breaks and chilly campsite
evenings it is the first thing most hikers pull out of the pack.
