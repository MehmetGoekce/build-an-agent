# Skyridge 2P Tent

- Product ID: TNT-001
- Category: Tents
- Price: CHF 359

A freestanding shelter for two people, built for backpackers who count
grams but still want room to sit out a storm. It pitches fast on almost any
surface because the poles support it without stakes, and it packs down to
2.2 kilograms split between two people.

Two doors mean nobody has to climb over a tent-mate for a midnight exit,
and two vestibules give each person dry space for boots and a pack. The
flysheet sheds rain and the mesh inner keeps insects out on warm nights.
