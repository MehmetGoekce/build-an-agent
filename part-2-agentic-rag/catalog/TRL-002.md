A large-volume pack for multi-day treks and expeditions, where you carry
everything you need on your back. Sixty-five litres swallows a sleeping
bag, a tent, a stove and several days of food, and the load rides close and
stable thanks to a harness that adjusts to your back length.

Padded hip-belt pockets keep snacks and a phone within reach, and external
straps lash on a rope or a sleeping mat. At 1.9 kilograms empty it is built
to be loaded heavy and stay comfortable hour after hour.
