# Nightfall -5°C Sleeping Bag

A three-season sleeping bag for campers who want a warm, comfortable night
when the temperature drops close to freezing. It is rated to a -5°C comfort
level, so spring, summer and autumn nights in the mountains are well within
its range.

Goose-down fill traps your body heat efficiently while keeping the packed
size small — it compresses to roughly the size of a loaf of bread and
weighs 1.1 kilograms. The mummy shape and a snug hood stop warmth leaking
out around your head and shoulders.
